## 1、创建新用户

1. useradd sqc
2. passwd 123456
3. groupadd gp1
4. usermod -G gp1 sqc

## 2、加入sudoers

1. vi /etc/sudoers
2. %gp1            ALL=(ALL)                ALL

## 3、分配工作空间

1. mkdir workplace 
2. chmod -R 764 workplace
3. chown gp1:sqc workplace
